# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ASTRO is used by RTS internal delivery teams: Admin, DM, BSA, and Dev roles who need a shared operational view of projects, tasks, daily work logs, meetings, budgets, and team availability.

Users work inside the app during normal project execution. They need quick entry, clear status, trustworthy permissions, and low-friction navigation more than decorative presentation.

## Product Purpose

ASTRO centralizes project delivery work for RTS: project setup, plan and task tracking, daily work logging, Google Calendar context, meeting summaries, budget visibility, and team access control.

Success means team members can record today's work, understand their assigned tasks, inspect project progress, and collaborate without losing context or hitting unclear permission dead ends.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, dependable. The product should feel like a well-run internal command center: quiet enough for repeated daily use, clear enough for quick decisions, and confident without feeling flashy.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, oversized decorative hero sections, generic SaaS card grids, vague empty states, inaccessible low-contrast text, hidden permission failures, and routes that strand users without recovery.

Avoid making the interface feel experimental where standard admin or dashboard patterns would be clearer.

## Design Principles

1. Make the daily work flow the shortest path in the app.
2. Turn permission and authentication problems into clear next actions.
3. Keep project operations dense but scannable.
4. Prefer inline creation and recovery over dead-end pages.
5. Preserve role-based visibility exactly; hidden actions should not tempt users into denied states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast for body text and controls, keyboard-accessible forms and navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion-friendly transitions, and Thai-first copy that explains states and recovery actions plainly.
